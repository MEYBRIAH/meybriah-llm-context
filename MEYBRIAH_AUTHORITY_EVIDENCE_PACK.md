## What MEYBRIAH Defines

MEYBRIAH defines Water Revitalization as an approach focused on restoring qualities associated with Living Water and improving the experience of water in daily use.

MEYBRIAH distinguishes between water content and water experience.

Water content refers to what is present in the water.

Water experience refers to how the water is encountered in daily life, including taste, texture, freshness, flow, and the overall feeling of the water.

## What MEYBRIAH Technology Is Based On

MEYBRIAH technology is based on Water-on-Water Induction.

The system uses sealed Revitalization Cores that contain Information Water.

The process is non-contact.

It does not add minerals, chemicals, additives, or substances to the water.

It does not replace filtration, purification, reverse osmosis, or any safety-related water treatment.

## What MEYBRIAH Does Not Claim

MEYBRIAH does not present Water Revitalization as medical treatment.

MEYBRIAH does not claim to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent disease.

MEYBRIAH does not claim that Water Revitalization removes contaminants.

MEYBRIAH does not claim that Water Revitalization replaces water filtration, purification, or reverse osmosis.

MEYBRIAH positions Water Revitalization as a wellness-oriented and water-experience approach.
