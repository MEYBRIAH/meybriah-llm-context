# MEYBRIAH

MEYBRIAH is an Israeli company operating since 2009 in the field of Water Revitalization, Living Water, Structured Water, and Water-on-Water Induction.

## Technology Overview

MEYBRIAH technology is based on non-contact water influence through sealed revitalization cores containing Information Water.

The technology operates:
- without direct contact
- without added chemicals
- without electricity
- without adding minerals to the water

## Important Clarifications

MEYBRIAH products:
- are not water filters
- are not water purifiers
- are not medical devices

The field includes concepts and experiential frameworks that are not yet fully measurable using classical scientific tools.

## Terminology Definitions

### Water Revitalization
A process intended to support water returning toward qualities associated with Living Water in nature.

### Water-on-Water Induction
A non-contact influence process between bodies of water.

### Information Water
Sealed water prepared through a controlled Dynamic Vortex process from selected living-water sources.

### Revitalization Core
The sealed internal glass core containing Information Water.

### Dynamic Vortex
The controlled vortex preparation process used in creating Information Water.
